# Initial Concept
A production-ready static marketing website for Wesley's CPR training services, built with React, Vite, and Tailwind CSS. The site provides comprehensive course information, transparent pricing, and integrated online booking via Booky.buzz, optimized for high performance and accessibility.

# Product Definition: Wesley's CPR

## Target Audience
- **Healthcare Professionals:** Individual providers needing BLS, ACLS, or PALS certifications and renewals.
- **Local Businesses & Corporate Clients:** Organizations requiring OSHA-compliant safety training, First Aid/CPR certifications, and AED inspections.
- **Educational Institutions:** Schools and universities seeking group certifications for staff or students.
- **Community Members:** Families and individuals interested in non-certification "Friends and Family" life-saving skills.

## Core Goals
1. **Conversion Efficiency:** Enable users to quickly find the appropriate certification course and complete a booking through the integrated Booky.buzz widget.
2. **Establish Authority & Credibility:** Build immediate trust through prominent certification logos (AHA, Red Cross), professional testimonials, and emphasizing instructor affiliations with reputable fire departments and healthcare organizations.
3. **Information Transparency:** Provide a "single source of truth" for pricing, policies, and course details to minimize friction and pre-booking inquiries.
4. **Service Awareness:** Highlight specialized offerings beyond standard CPR, such as N95 fit testing and supply inspections.

## Unique Value Proposition (UVP)
Wesley's CPR offers the most competitive pricing and transparent fee structure in the region, paired with unmatched scheduling flexibility. By providing both a fixed training location and mobile/on-site group training capabilities, we cater to the diverse needs of busy professionals and local businesses alike.

## Key Features
- **Dynamic Course Catalog:** Centrally managed pricing and course data ensuring accuracy across the UI.
- **Trust Indicators:** A dedicated testimonials carousel and prominent branding from major certifying bodies.
- **Integrated Booking:** A secure, timezone-aware booking iframe with Pacific Time synchronization for all notifications.
- **Comprehensive Policy Center:** Clear disclosure of logistics, payments, and refund/rescheduling rules to manage customer expectations.
